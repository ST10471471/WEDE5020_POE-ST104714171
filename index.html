The two brothers, Mr. L. Watts and Mr. S. Watts founded Fragrance World Perfumes in South Africa in 2024 with the goal of becoming a leading organization in the fragrance market. Our aim is to produce and offer luxurious, stronger scents that attract and satisfy both local and foreign consumers. Fragrance World Perfumes creates a wide range of outstanding fragrances. Ensuring that there is a perfect, premium fragrance to fit every occasion and meet the unique preferences of each and every one of the customers we serve.
